Motion Videos

Candy Corn Construction

The intent of this piece was to create a looping gif. I aimed for playful content and bright colour use to make it a fun and interesting piece. I tried to keep it clean though to as it is simply a basic gif. I use Illustrator to create the piece, After Effects to animate it, and Photoshop to make it into a gif.

Peril in Parallax City

This video looks fun and interesting, while incorporating the parallax function in After Effects. The assets and artwork was created using Illustrator, while the rest was animated with After Effects using basic keyframes and effects to create the "shaking" of the buildings.

Bagel Boy Takes a Stroll

This video incorporated strong aesthetic with experimentation using a walk cycle animation. I used Photoshop to create the background, Illustrator for the characters, and After Effects to bring it all together.
